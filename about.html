About Carl

A hometown attorney with world-class experience.

Hi folks! My name is Carl Clyde

I'm a husband of 10 years, a dad, and a Grants Pass native - actually born and raised here. I went to undergraduate school in Santa Barbara, law school at the University of Iowa, and still ended up raising my own family in our hometown. Go Cavemen!

That journey - from Grants Pass to Santa Barbara to Iowa and back home - taught me something important: there's no place like home, and there's no work more meaningful than serving the community that raised you.

Education & Background

  • University of Iowa College of Law
  • Pepperdine School of Law London Program
  • Former Partner / Attorney at Sorenson, Ransom, Ferguson & Clyde LLP

Why I Practice Law Here

When I started Valor Law, I made a commitment: to be the kind of lawyer this community deserves. That means being accessible, honest, and genuinely invested in your success. I'm not here to bill hours or complicate things - I'm here to help you navigate life's important legal decisions with clarity and care.
