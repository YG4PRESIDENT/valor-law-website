About Carl

Hi folks! My name is Carl Clyde

I'm a husband of 10 years, a dad, and a Grants Pass native—actually born and raised here. I managed to go to undergraduate school in Santa Barbara, law school in London, and still ended up raising my own family in our hometown. Go Cavemen!

That journey—from Grants Pass to Santa Barbara to London and back home—taught me something important: there's no place like home, and there's no work more meaningful than serving the community that raised you.

Why I Practice Law Here

When I started Valor Law in 2021, I made a commitment: to be the kind of lawyer this community deserves. That means being accessible, honest, and genuinely invested in your success. I'm not here to bill hours or complicate things—I'm here to help you navigate life's important legal decisions with clarity and care.

Whether you're planning your estate, dealing with a probate matter, or facing a real property issue, you'll work directly with me. No layers of associates, no corporate bureaucracy. Just straightforward legal guidance from someone who understands your community because I'm part of it.

My Practice Areas

I focus on Trusts and Estates Planning, Probates and Trust Administration, and Real Property and Landlord/Tenant Matters. These areas touch on some of life's most important moments—protecting your family's future, handling a loved one's estate, or managing property matters. I approach each case with the respect and attention it deserves.
