Trusts & Estates Planning

Planning for the future isn't just about protecting your assets—it's about protecting your family. A well-crafted estate plan ensures your wishes are honored and your loved ones are taken care of, even when you're no longer here to guide them.

I work with families to create comprehensive estate plans that include:

  • Wills - Clearly document your wishes for asset distribution
  • Trusts - Protect assets and provide for beneficiaries according to your specific goals
  • Power of Attorney - Designate someone to handle your affairs if you're unable
  • Healthcare Directives - Ensure your medical wishes are respected
  • Estate Tax Planning - Minimize tax burdens for your heirs

Every family is different, and your estate plan should reflect that. I take the time to understand your unique situation and goals, then create a plan that works for you—no cookie-cutter solutions, no unnecessary complexity.

Probates & Trust Administration

Losing a loved one is difficult enough without having to navigate the legal complexities of probate or trust administration. During this challenging time, you need compassionate guidance and clear communication.

I help families through the probate and trust administration process, handling:

  • Probate Administration - Guide executors through the court-supervised process of settling an estate
  • Trust Administration - Assist trustees in fulfilling their duties and distributing trust assets
  • Estate Litigation - Resolve disputes that may arise during probate or administration
  • Asset Distribution - Ensure assets are distributed according to the will or trust terms
  • Creditor Claims - Handle creditor notifications and claims properly

My goal is to make this process as smooth and stress-free as possible for you and your family. I'll handle the legal details so you can focus on what matters most—honoring your loved one's memory and supporting each other.

Real Property & Landlord/Tenant Matters

Whether you're buying your first home, managing rental properties, or dealing with a landlord-tenant dispute, real property matters require careful attention to detail and clear communication.

I provide legal guidance for a wide range of real property matters:

  • Real Estate Transactions - Review contracts, handle closings, and ensure your interests are protected
  • Landlord Representation - Draft leases, handle evictions, and resolve tenant disputes
  • Tenant Rights - Protect your rights as a tenant and resolve disputes with landlords
  • Property Disputes - Resolve boundary issues, easements, and other property conflicts
  • Title Issues - Address title defects, liens, and other ownership problems

Real property is often one of your most significant assets. I'll help you navigate these matters with expertise and care, ensuring your rights are protected and your transactions go smoothly.
